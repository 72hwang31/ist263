Nephilim

Story

In ancient times, a group of angels known as the Watchers was sent to Earth to watch humanity. Instead of doing their duty, the angels mingled with human women and taught them all kinds of technology and knowledge like metalwork, magic, medicine, astrology, etc, which led the humans into sin. The women gave birth to the Nephilim, who grew so large that humans couldn't sustain them so they ate humans and all kinds of animals, eventually causing the world to be turned into chaos. Seeing this, God decided to start the Great Flood and kill all Nephilim and humans except Noah.

Origins

  • Jewish Esoteric Tradition

Mention in:

  • Book of Enoch

Estimated Height

According to the Book of Enoch, the Nephilim were three thousand ells tall. One ell is about 18 inches. This would mean that they were 4,500 feet/1,370 meters tall. That is the size of a small mountain.
