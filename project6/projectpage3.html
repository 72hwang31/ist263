Cormoran

Story

Cormoran was a giant who lived in St Michael’s Mount in Cornwall. For years, he terrorized the residents of Cornwall by forcibly eating their cattle alive, and taking away their sheep and hogs back to his cave. One day, a young boy named Jack forcibly woke him from his sleep. Enraged, Cormoran chased Jack but fell into a pit that the boy had previously dug. Trapped by his own size, Cormoran remained helpless as Jack killed him with a pickaxe.

Origins

Mention in:

Estimated Height

Cormoran was stated to be 18 feet/5.5 meters tall and 3 yards wide. That is more than three times the average height of an adult man and almost as tall as an adult giraffe. A noteworthy size but not towering over cities.
